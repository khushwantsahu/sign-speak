[1:00 — ARCHITECTURE]
(show assets/architecture_diagram.png)
Let's look at the system architecture.
First, user questions pass through our security checkpoint to block prompt injections, scrub PII like email and credit cards, and filter inappropriate requests.
Next, our topic collector acts as a human-in-the-loop pause, dynamically asking the user what area they want to focus on: gestures, phrases, or practice.
Once captured, the sign_orchestrator routes the request to our specialized sub-agents: gesture_coach, phrase_coach, and practice_partner.
These sub-agents communicate with our local MCP Server, utilizing tools to retrieve official hand descriptions, perform dictionary searches, generate mnemonics, and format interactive quizzes.
